My name is Kevin Mathews

Hi there! I'm Kevin Mathews, from the San Francisco Bay Area. Coding has been a passion from of mine since I started teaching myself to program in basic on a T1994A Texas Instruments computer when I was just 7 year old. I have a lot of passions in life from Basketball, to the UFC, to just enjoying the 'Dad' life and trying to be the best husband and father I can be.

I've worn a lot of hats in my life, and not only am I a web developer, but I have a background in sales and entrepreneurship. As a business owner I learned how to handle and resolve a wide range of issues which now enables me to approach projects with confidence and creativity. I bring my passion for a clean and organized work product into each project, and I love nothing more then working as a team to find the most efficient and elegant solutions to complex problems.
